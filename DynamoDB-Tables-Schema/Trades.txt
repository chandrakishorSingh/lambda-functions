- Fields
	- PhoneNumber : (String)
	- Trades : (Array of TradeItem)
	- TradeItem : (Map) it has following fields.
		- Symbol : (String)
		- SignalType : (String) either 'buy' or 'sell'
		- CreatedAt : (String) date in YYYY-MM-DD format
		- Quantity : (Number)
		- Price : (Number)
		- TradeAmount : (Number)
