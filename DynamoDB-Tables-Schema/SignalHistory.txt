- Fields
	- Symbol: (String) represents the NSE symbol of stock(in uppercase)
	- ClosePrice: (Number) last close price of stock
	- CloseDate: (String) date in YYYY-MM-DD format
	- GainEWMA: (Number) exponential weighted moving average of stock price gains
	- LossEWMA: (Number) exponential weighted moving average of stock price losses
	- RsiArr: (Number[]) array of last 13 RSI. in chronological order. latest will be the last.
	- StochRsiArr: (Number[]) array of last 2 stochastic array. in chronological order. latest will be the last.
	- FastkArr: (Number[]) array of last 2 FastK values. in chronological order. latest will be the last.
