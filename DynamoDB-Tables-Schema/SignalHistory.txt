- Fields
	- Symbol: (String) represents the NSE symbol of stock(in uppercase)
	- ClosePrice: (Number) last close price of stock
	- CloseDate: (Number) date in ms representing last date when stock was traded
	- GainEWMA: (Number) exponential weighted moving average of stock price gains
	- LossEWMA: (Number) exponential weighted moving average of stock price losses
	- RsiArr: (Number[]) array of last 13 RSI
	- StochRsiArr: (Number[]) array of last 2 stochastic array
	- FastkArr: (Number[]) array of last 2 FastK values
